Hi, I’m Vasu Sharma 🙂

I build multimodal foundation models that power generative AI agents. Currently, I lead applied research at Meta AI (FAIR), where my work spans training & scaling 100B‑parameter LLMs (Llama 3/4), early‑fusion models like Chameleon, and state‑of‑the‑art self‑supervised vision systems (DINOv2).

My broader interests include computer vision, NLP, audio‑video representation learning, agentic workflows and evaluation & safety of large models.
